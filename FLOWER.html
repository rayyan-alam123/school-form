About Rose

The rose plant is one of the best varieties of flowers in the world. The rose plant is an ornamental plant. People plant it in their house pots due to its beauty and smell. The different colours of roses are red, pink and white.

About Lotus

Lotus is the national flower of India. It is very beautiful and fragrant in appearance. The most common colour is pink in lotus flowers. However, some purple coloured lotuses are also found.

About Lily

The lily flower is beautiful and aromatic in appearance. It is noted for its beauty, aroma, and shape. Its flower petals have brownish or pinkish lines on the outside.

About Tulip

Tulip is a flowering plant in spring. This beautiful flower is found all over the world. There is a complete lack of smell in this flower. The look of tulip in enchanting, pure and mixed red, golden, and purple shades captivates the audience.
